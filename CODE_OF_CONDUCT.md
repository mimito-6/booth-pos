# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## TL;DR

OpenBooth is built for and by indie creators — many of whom make fan-work, adult content, or otherwise sensitive material. **We respect that and so do you.**

- Be kind. Disagree without insulting.
- Don't moralize about what other people sell. (Illegal content is the only exception.)
- Don't out anyone's real identity, sales numbers, or customer data.
- Don't redistribute someone else's fan-work / IP-based preset publicly.

## Reporting

If someone makes the community unsafe for you, contact the maintainer privately on GitHub (`@mimito-6`) or by email at `hohoho0626 [at] gmail [dot] com`. Reports are kept confidential.

The maintainer may warn, mute, ban, or remove content at their discretion.
